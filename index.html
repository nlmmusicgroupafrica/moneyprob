About Money Prob

Discover our journey and commitment to South African culture

Our Heritage

Money Prob was born from a passion for South African culture and a desire to share it with the world. Our founder, Thando Mbatha, started this company with a vision to create clothing that celebrates the vibrant diversity of South African traditions.

Each garment we produce tells a story of South Africa's rich cultural tapestry, from the vibrant Shweshwe fabrics of the Eastern Cape to the intricate beadwork of KwaZulu-Natal.

We believe in fashion that means something - that connects wearers to the stories, people, and traditions behind each piece.
